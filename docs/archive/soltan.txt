We are pleased to invite you to a presentation by Professor Jafar Soltan on the
Opportunities for Collaboration with the College of Engineering, University of
Saskatchewan.
Professor Soltan holds a Ph.D. in Chemical Engineering from the University of British
Columbia and is a Professional Engineer and Fellow of Engineers Canada. With over
25 years of industrial and academic experience in energy, environmental sustainability,
and catalytic reactions, his research focuses on developing technologies for the
degradation of emerging pollutants in water and the treatment of polluted indoor air.
Currently, he serves as the Associate Dean of Research and Partnerships at the
College of Engineering, University of Saskatchewan.
In his presentation, Professor Soltan will provide an overview of the University of
Saskatchewan, with a particular focus on the College of Engineering, and explore
opportunities for research collaboration and joint academic programs.
